## Purpose

This section documents the risks inherent in the design, any exceptions to policies or standards, and the governance traceability that demonstrates the design has been reviewed and approved.

## 6.1 Design Risks & Mitigations

<span class="maturity-indicator maturity-minimum">Minimum</span>

Document all identified design risks and their mitigation plans:

| Risk # | Risk Event / Issue | Mitigation / Remediation Plan | Residual Risk | Target Date | Owner |

| R-001 | *[describe the risk]* | *[tactical and/or strategic plan]* | High / Medium / Low | *[date or acceptance]* | *[person]* |

<div class="guidance-box">
<h4>Guidance</h4>

Consider risks related to:
- **Technical** - Technology choices, complexity, integration, performance
- **Security** - Vulnerabilities, access control gaps, data exposure
- **Operational** - Supportability, monitoring gaps, skill availability
- **Delivery** - Timeline, dependencies, resource availability
- **Commercial** - Vendor risk, licensing, cost overruns
- **Compliance** - Regulatory gaps, standards non-conformance
</div>
